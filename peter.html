roblox

Roblox, is an online game platform and game creation system developed by Roblox Corporation that allows users to program and play games created by themselves or other users.

Roblox has over 70 million daily active users worldwide. As of March 2024, Roblox's monthly active users were 196 million. The European region has the largest number of Roblox daily active users at 26.9%. Brookhaven RP is named the most popular game on Roblox with 34.44 billion visitors.
